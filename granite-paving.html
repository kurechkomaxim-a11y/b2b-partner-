Granite paving

Granite paving — sourced from the right country for your project.

Cubes, slabs, steps, coping, and kerbs — across four sourcing countries. We match stone, format, finish, and lead time to what the project actually needs.

What we source

Core formats, proven finishes.

Paving cubes

Natursteinpflaster

Standard formats: 100×100×50, 200×100×80, 100×100×100 mm
Finishes: split, flamed, sawn, bush-hammered
Typical use: pedestrian streets, squares, driveways, heritage work

Paving slabs

Bodenplatten

Standard formats: 300×600, 400×400, 600×400, custom
Thicknesses: 20, 30, 40, 60 mm
Finishes: flamed, honed, sawn, bush-hammered
Typical use: public spaces, commercial outdoor, landscape projects

Kerbs, steps, coping

Bordsteine & elements

Formats: per specification
Finishes: sawn, flamed, rock-faced
Typical use: project completion elements alongside paving

Sourcing countries

Four countries, chosen for the project — not the other way around.

01 · Ukraine

Core source for grey, black, red granites

Pokostovka, Gabbro, Labradorite.

Strengths: direct factory access, EN-compliant documentation, short lead time for Europe.

Typical lead time: 5–7 weeks door-to-site.

02 · China

Broadest format and finish range

G603, G654, G684, and custom colours.

Strengths: volume capacity, custom formats, consistent quality tiers.

Typical lead time: 10–12 weeks door-to-site.

03 · Vietnam

Basalt and specific granites

Strong alternative for dark, hard-wearing paving.

Strengths: underpriced vs. equivalent Chinese material, consistent quality.

Typical lead time: 9–11 weeks door-to-site.

04 · Egypt

Red and pink granites

Aswan, Red Forsan, New Halayeb.

Strengths: short Mediterranean shipping to southern Europe.

Typical lead time: 7–9 weeks door-to-site.

Selection logic

How we pick the right source for a project.

01

Specification fit

Does the stone meet the brief's colour, strength, finish, and tolerance?

02

Landed cost

Factory price plus freight, customs, and our fee.

03

Lead time

Including buffer for realistic timing — not factory-promised timing.

04

Risk profile

Supplier track record on this format, at this volume, to this market.

Two to three options are presented so you can see the trade-offs rather than take our word on one.
