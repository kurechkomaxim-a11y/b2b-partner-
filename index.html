Who this is for

Built for teams whose project needs stone — not for stone traders.

We work with

  • Landscape contractors and GaLaBau teams
  • Outdoor construction and public-space builders
  • Mid-sized general contractors on project-based stone demand

We don't work with

  • Stone yards and traders buying for resale
  • Buyers looking for commodity warehouse stock without support
  • Projects where delivery, documents, and quality control aren't important
What we solve

Stone procurement, without building a procurement function.

01

Category expertise

We know granite paving as a category — formats, finishes, tolerances, packaging, behaviour on site. You don't need to learn it to get the right material.

02

Landed price, not list price

Our quote includes the factory price, freight, customs, and our fee — itemised. You see what you're paying for, and where.

03

One responsible process

Sourcing, inspection, logistics, customs, documents — one counterpart, one timeline, one point of accountability.

04

Project-based, not catalogue-based

We work from your project brief or BOQ, not from a shelf. If the right answer is two sources instead of one, we configure it that way.

How it works

Four steps, 48 hours to the first sourcing options.

01 · Day 0

Project brief

You send the specification, BOQ, or drawings. We clarify volume, finish, tolerances, delivery point, and target timing.

02 · Day 1–2

2–3 sourcing options

We return two to three realistic paths — typically across different source countries — with factory-level pricing and lead time.

03 · After selection

Landed quote + documents

Full landed price including freight, customs, and our fee. Sample agreement, SLA, and payment milestones defined upfront.

04 · Execution

Coordinated delivery

Pre-shipment inspection, logistics, customs clearance, and delivery to your site — with the full document pack at each checkpoint.
